Privacy Policy

qBittorrent WebUI
Last Updated: January 13, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how qBittorrent WebUI ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifier: A unique device ID generated when you first install the app
  • Trial Information: Trial start date and status (active/expired)
  • Purchase Information: Purchase tokens from Google Play for verification only

User-Provided Information

  • qBittorrent Server Credentials: Server URL, username, and password
  • Biometric Data (Optional): Stored locally using Android Keystore

How We Use Your Information

  • Trial Management: Track your 3-day free trial period
  • Purchase Verification: Verify legitimate purchases through Google Play
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Rate limiting and device validation

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage (On Your Device)

  • qBittorrent server credentials
  • Biometric authentication data (Android Keystore)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Trial status cache

Cloud Storage (Cloudflare Workers KV)

  • Device ID (anonymized identifier)
  • Trial start date
  • Purchase status and token
  • Rate limiting information

Security Measures

  • All data transmitted using HTTPS encryption
  • Cloudflare KV data encrypted at rest
  • Rate limiting to prevent unauthorized access
  • Device ID validation to prevent fraud

Third-Party Services

Services We Use

  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchases and payment processing
  • Cloudflare Workers: Backend API for trial and purchase verification
  • @capgo/native-purchases: Google Play Billing integration library

Services We Do NOT Use

  • Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • Advertising networks
  • Crash reporting with personal data
  • Social media integrations
  • Data brokers or third-party marketers

Data Retention

  • Trial Data: Retained indefinitely for fraud prevention
  • Purchase Data: Retained indefinitely to honor lifetime purchases
  • Login Credentials: Stored locally until you uninstall the app
  • Rate Limiting Data: Auto-deleted after 60 minutes

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your trial and purchase data
  • Request deletion of your device ID (contact us)
  • Opt out of biometric authentication
  • Uninstall the app (removes all local data)

Note: Deleting your device ID will reset your trial period and may affect your purchase status.

Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your qBittorrent credentials with anyone
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Track your torrent activity

We DO:

  • Store minimal device information for trial tracking
  • Use Google Play for payment processing
  • Use Cloudflare for secure backend services

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app and on our website.
